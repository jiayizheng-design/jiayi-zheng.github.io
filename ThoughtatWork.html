About Thought at Work

Thought at work is a student-run design conference for students. I was in the graphic team and was responsible to create the posters, graphics for merchandise, and the brochure based on the branding guide of the year. My idea of creating the graphics was enhancing the playful part of the style by playing with different perspective and layouts.
